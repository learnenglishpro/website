Exam Preparation

Get help preparing for your English exam with one of our tutors. You can get exam preparation for IELTS, TOEFL, TOEIC, CAE, FCE, PET, and BEC. To ensure your tutor is capable of preparing for your exam, look for the corresponding exam icon on the tutor's profile:

IELTS The International English Testing System
TOEFL Test of English as a Foreign Language
TOEIC Test of English for International Communication
CAE Cambridge Advanced English
FCE First Certificate English
PET Preliminary English Test
BEC Cambridge English Business Certificates

If you have other English exams you would like help with, please send your exam material to our tutor before the lesson to have our tutor help you with your exam preparation.
